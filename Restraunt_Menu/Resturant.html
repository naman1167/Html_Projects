About Swad Mahal

Swad Mahal is a culinary destination where tradition meets taste. Located in the heart of Uttar Pradesh, we bring you the authentic flavors of India with a royal touch. Our chefs use the freshest ingredients and age-old recipes to create a thali experience that’s truly anubhav! Whether you’re here with family or friends, Swad Mahal promises a warm ambience and unforgettable hospitality.
