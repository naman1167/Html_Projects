Rise in Popularity

Blogging gained popularity in 1999 with platforms like Open Diary and LiveJournal. Open Diary was the first to allow reader comments, creating blog communities.

By the mid-2000s, blogs became essential media platforms, with political blogs, fashion blogs, tech blogs, and personal blogs dominating the web. Today, millions of blog posts are published daily.
